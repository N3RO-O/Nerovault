Nerovault Help

What is Nerovault?

Nerovault is a completely free, private budget tracker. Everything you enter stays on your device — no accounts, no sign-ups, no cloud uploads. Use it to track your income, expenses, bills, and savings goals.

Getting started

  1. Set your monthly budget at the top of the Budget categories panel.
  2. Click Add to log your first income, expense, bill, or savings goal.
  3. Watch your stats update instantly — available to spend, upcoming bills, total saved, and your daily budget.
  4. Customize your budget categories — add, edit percentages, or delete them as you like.

Budget presets

The Presets chips above your categories instantly split your budget across the four groups — Family & Household, Essential Survival, Personal & Lifestyle, and Future & Savings. Tap any chip to apply it; it redistributes your current categories automatically (fixed-₱ categories are left alone).

Eight starter presets are included (50/30/20, 70/20/10, 60/30/10, 80/10/10, and more). Tap + New to save your own split — edit or delete your custom presets anytime with the small icons on each chip.

Income

Log any money you receive — salary, allowance, freelance pay, gifts. Turn on Repeat monthly when adding it and Nerovault will add it again automatically at the start of each new month, so you don't have to re-enter it.

Expenses

Every expense belongs to a category. The category progress bars show how much of that category's budget you've used so far this month.

  • Under 90% used — on track
  • 90–99% used — almost at your limit
  • 100%+ used — over budget for that category

Bills

Schedule bills with due dates. Each one moves through three stages automatically:

Not yet due → Ready to pay → Paid

A bill becomes Ready to pay on its due date — tap Mark as paid once you've paid it. Recurring bills auto-generate each month. Use the sort dropdown to order the list by due date or amount.

Savings Goals

Set a target amount and an optional deadline for something specific — a trip, a gadget, an emergency fund. Add funds to it whenever you like. Nerovault shows your pace: roughly how long you'll take to reach the goal at your current saving rate. Hit the target and you get a confetti celebration 🎉

Savings Ledger

A separate running record for general saving that isn't tied to one specific goal — think of it as your overall "set aside" pile. Entries are grouped by month. The Savings tab shows what you've added this month (with a trend vs. last month) and your all-time Running Balance.

Net Worth & Savings Rate

These two numbers answer two different questions:

  • Net Worth — "How much cash do I actually have?" Everything you've earned, minus everything you've spent and every bill (paid or upcoming).
  • Savings Rate — "What share of my income am I setting aside?" Money in Goals and the Savings Ledger, as a percentage of total income.

Example: you received ₱20,000.00 and your expenses plus bills add up to ₱12,000.00. Your Net Worth is ₱8,000.00 — money set aside in Goals or the Savings Ledger is still counted as yours, so it isn't subtracted.

Formula, if you want it: Net Worth = Income − (Expenses + Paid Bills + Upcoming Bills). Savings Rate = (Goals + Savings Ledger) ÷ Income × 100.

Budget Rollover

Off by default. Turn it on in Settings and any budget you don't spend this month carries forward into next month instead of resetting — useful if you're saving up for something bigger than one month's budget.

Backup & Restore

All your data lives only in this browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere. That also means clearing your browser data or switching devices/browsers will lose it, unless you back up first.

  1. Open the ⚙️ icon in the header → Backup & Restore.
  2. Download backup saves a .json file — keep it somewhere safe (cloud drive, email to yourself, etc).
  3. To restore, open the same panel on any device and choose that file.

Tip: back up right after making a lot of changes, and again every so often.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Press N to open the Add modal, ? for help, Esc to close modals.

Frequently asked questions

Why is my "Available" amount negative?
You've spent more than your budget allocates for this month (or a category is over its limit). It's not an error — it's telling you spending has outpaced budget.

Does changing currency convert my amounts?
No. It only changes the symbol and number formatting shown. A "20,000" doesn't become a different real amount — Nerovault doesn't do currency conversion.

I cleared my browser history/cache — where did my data go?
Since everything is stored locally in the browser, clearing site data removes it. This is why regular backups matter (see above).

Can I use this on more than one device?
Each browser/device keeps its own separate data. Use Backup & Restore to move data between them — there's no automatic sync.

Can I install this as an app?
Yes. On Android/desktop Chrome or Edge, tap the Install app button in the header (or the install icon in the address bar). On iPhone/iPad, open this page in Safari, tap the Share icon, then Add to Home Screen — Safari doesn't support one-tap install buttons.
